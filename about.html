Svasta-Seva is a innovative approach of keeping track of Hospitals and all of it's important resources.
But do not get confused thinking Svasta-Seva is just a tracker, it is much more than that.
Svasta-Seva optimises the workflow of the Hospital taking care of things behind the curtains.

Svasta-Seva has both Hardware and Software elements.

  • The hardware consists of sensoring mechanism that checks the occupancy of the bed and quantity of resources available and it will relay this information to the database in realtime
  • On the software side the database will collect all the data and depending on the user (admin or public user) it will be available to them.
  • The admin will be able to keep track of information seamlessly with the Svasta-Seva App where the data will be presented in a meaningful way to the user
  • The public users will be able to see the bed occupancy and other relevant data related to the particular hospital. Hence, the user can make an informed decision about their treatment and book appointments among other things

Svasta-Seva is a complete ecosystem which revolutionizes the digital system of the hospital to a whole new level maintaining the personal touch
